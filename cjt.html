About the App

Chandan Ji Team is a comprehensive worker management application designed specifically for businesses that need to track workers, attendance, daily wages (dehari), and payments. Built with simplicity and efficiency in mind, this app helps you streamline your workforce management operations, maintain accurate records, and generate detailed reports. Whether you're managing a construction site, manufacturing unit, or any business with daily wage workers, CJT provides all the tools you need in one secure platform.
